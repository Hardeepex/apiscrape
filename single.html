Overview

Spring Valley Estates is a new single family home community By Pearl Valley Homes Inc. currently in preconstruction at 1328 Queen Street West, Brampton. The community is scheduled for completion in 2024. Available units start from $1,650,000. Spring Valley Estates has a total of 36 units. Sizes range from 2600 to 5600 square feet.
